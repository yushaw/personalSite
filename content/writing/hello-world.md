This is the beginning.

I build AI products. I've been writing design documents, research notes, and architectural decision records for a long time -- but mostly for myself and the projects I work on.

Now I'm writing here. Not to teach, not to market -- but to think in public. Product design decisions, engineering trade-offs, industry analysis, and the messy reality of shipping software.

## What to expect

I'll write about:

- **AI product design** -- how to design memory systems, permission models, plugin architectures for AI applications
- **Engineering decisions** -- why SQLite over Postgres, how to integrate MCP, when to build vs. buy
- **Industry analysis** -- what competitors are doing, reverse engineering interesting implementations
- **Building in public** -- project management, requirement management, the craft of shipping

No schedule. No SEO optimization. Just honest writing about the work.
